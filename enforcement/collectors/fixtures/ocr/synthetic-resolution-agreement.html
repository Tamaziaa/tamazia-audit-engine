HHS' Office for Civil Rights Settles HIPAA Security Rule Investigation with Example Health Group LLC

July 10, 2026

The HHS Office for Civil Rights (OCR) announced today a settlement with Example Health Group LLC, a fictional healthcare provider used for testing, resolving potential violations of the Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act of 1996 (HIPAA) Security Rule following a ransomware investigation affecting 12,345 individuals.

Under the terms of the resolution agreement, Example Health Group LLC paid $75,000 to OCR and agreed to implement a corrective action plan that OCR will monitor for two years.
